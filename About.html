About

PO1 AR-15: A Cyberpunk Bartending Webcomic, is made by a beginner artist (that's me! hello!!!) fueled by a passionate obsession over VA-11 HALL-A: Cyberpunk Bartending Action, an amazing Booze 'em Up (It's a visual novel) made by Sukeban Games.

Following the same structure as Va-11 Hall-A, Polaris (Yes, this is how you're supposed to read the name.) features a bartender as the protagonist and plenty of different characters ready to tear their hearts out over a drink and a quiet moment in that hellish city they all live in. As the conversation flows, drinks will be made and I plan on educating my readers on how to make various cocktails so you too can roleplay as a cool and mysterious bartender at home!

I promised myself I'd work on it diligently during 2024, but if everything goes well, I'll be drawing it for the next few years. I'm not very good at art at the moment, but that's exactly why I want to do this! I've seen artists grow immensely while working on a webcomic, and I hope I too can improve a lot over time. Plus, an ugly comic is far better than no comic at all!
