ABOUT

Hiya! I'm Max, and I hope you've enjoyed my silly website so far!

I'm a 21-year-old Portuguese queer in an eternal battle between the crimpling anxiety of my inevitable decay and the limits of my brain that only let me do a few things before having to deal with burnout.

After choosing STEM over a career in music, I moved to a new city for college and lost most connections to what had been my main source of artistic fulfillment. I still went to jam sessions, but I didn't have my drum kit, and all my band members had gone to different places so we effectively separated. I was basically very sad. I filled the gaps with creative writing and TTRPGs but 3 years ago I decided to buy a Huion H640P and learn how to draw. I was told as a kid that I didn't have any talent for it, but I'm pretty sure you can get good at anything with enough hard work, and that's what I'm doing.

It's going well so far!

I made POLARIS to learn the basics of comic making, and I made a website cuz having a website is hella fun and I didn't feel like posting it in a proper place until I had enough episodes. It started off as a simple place, but I wanted it to be silly and fun so after over 120 hours working on it, here we are! Honestly, I'm really proud of it.

Max is dreaming and she must wake up. For a clock keeps ticking, the race has begun, and a Jack of all trades, master of none, is oftentimes merrier than the master of one.
